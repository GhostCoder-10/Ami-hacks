Rules & Regulations

  • Each participant should be above 18 year of age.
  • Each participant must be present 24 Hours in the campus during the event.
  • Any case of misconduct/indiscipline shall directly lead to disqualification of the team.
  • In case of disqualification or unavailability of team in the campus, no refund will be provided to the participants.
  • The organizing institution shall only provide internet connectivity, i.e., no demand for additional software and hardware shall be entertained.
  • Participants shall bring their own devices including laptops, chargers, USB cables or stationary items.
  • Consumption of alcoholic/narcotics including usage of E-cigarettes is strictly not allowed during the event.
  • All participants shall abide by the rules of discipilne and conduct as set by Amity University Uttar Pradesh.
  • Organizing committee Ami-Hacks '24 reserves right to cancel the event or not to declare any result in particular category/theme.
  • If any member of team leaves campus during the event, organizing team will disqualify the team.

Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is CyberCup 3.0 is happening?

CyberCup 3.0 will be held from 9 a.m., 13th October, till 9 a.m., 14th October, at Amity University Uttar Pradesh, Noida campus, Sector 125, Noida, Uttar Pradesh.

Who can participate in this event?

Students of age above 18 associated with any university/college can participate in this event.

Are there any pre-requisite skills required to be eligible for participation?

Basic coding and problem solving skills required.

Do I need a team?

Students can participate either individually or in a team of maximum size 4.

Do I need to pay anything to participate in an event?

A registration fee of Rs 500/- per team for CyberCup 3.0.

Can I also join remotely?

Students cannot participate remotely as the rounds of evaluation will be held at Amity University Uttar Pradesh, Noida campus from 9 a.m., 13th October 2023, till 9 a.m., 14th October 2023

How to register for the event?

Click on the register link given above or follow the given link: https://www.amity.edu/aset/CyberCup2023/

How do I get regular updates about the events?

To get regular updates follow our socials given below. Moreover, Team Leaders will be added to Whatsapp Group within 2-3 days after registering for the event.

What's in it for me?

CyberCup 3.0 is a 24hr hackathon where students will have a chance to participate with other students across different universities/colleges, win exciting cash prizes, certificate of participations, awesome goodies and swags and be a part of an energizing yoga session held during the 24 hour event and work real world problems.
